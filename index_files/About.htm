Outdoor Living Concepts

About Us

After nearly two decades of success in building premium outdoor spaces for his Pacific Northwest clientele with his first entrepreneurial venture Custom Design Decks and Patios Ben Smith started Outdoor Living Concepts to give him an opportunity to bring his craft to the masses.

In this small, close-knit company quality and customer service is job one, this philosophy contributes to its on-going growth and success. By focusing on the customer and offering only the very finest, highest quality professional products, Outdoor Living Concepts will continue its aggressive growth and expansion.

Outdoor Living Concepts is a privately held company located in the Pacific Northwest which manufactures and markets the industry's finest custom outdoor grill islands, outdoor fireplaces and fire-pits. Through its relationship with market leaders like Viking and DCS they are able to deliver a premium, custom and complete outdoor experience throughout North America.
